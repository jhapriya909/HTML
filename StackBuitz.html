Our Values

Think Big

Thinking small is a self-fulfilling prophecy. We are working to change the future of web development and this big vision inspires our daily decisions.

Frugality

Accomplish more with less. Constraints breed resourcefulness, self-sufficiency, and invention. There are no extra points for growing headcount, budget size, or fixed expense.

Bias for Action

Speed matters in business. Many decisions and actions are reversible and do not need extensive study. We value calculated risk taking.

Deliver Results

Each team member focuses on their must-win tasks and completes them with the right quality and in a timely fashion. Despite setbacks, they rise to the occasion and never settle.
